College Tours iPhone Application

Like many high school juniors, I visited a variety of colleges up and down the eat coast accompanied by my parents. When it came time to apply to schools, however, I couldn't really remember what each college was like

During an iOS development class in 2011, my friend Josh and I wanted to help solve this problem. We created an app where a student/parent could rate each college on categories such as dorms, food, academics, athletics, social life, college town, etc.

  • Rating

    A simple interface where prospective students could give different aspects of the college ratings as they walked around

  • Impression

    Gave users the ability to summarize their overall impression of the visit.

  • Summary

    Interactive view to explore ratings of visited colleges

  • Share

    One touch share button allowed users to email information about each college to advisors, parents, friends, etc.

The app, College Tours, was on free the Apple App Store for a year and recieved well over 10,000 downloads from more then 20 countries. I have future ambitions for the App -- specifically uploading the data to a server so people can see what other people though of the college and data for each college could be aggregated as a whole
